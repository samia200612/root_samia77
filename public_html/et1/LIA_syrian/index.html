LIA- Traditional Syrian House

Sketch

Planner for 3 weeks

Week 1: Modeling

Start by collecting reference images to understand the design of traditional Syrian houses. Build the basic structure of the house, including walls, arches, doors, and windows. Add key details like a courtyard, mashrabiya, and a fountain. Work on the interior layout with simple furniture and room designs.

Week 2: Texturing and Lighting

Apply realistic textures to walls, floors, and furniture. Focus on traditional materials like stone, wood, and tiles. Set up lighting to create a natural and warm atmosphere, using HDRIs or manual light sources to mimic daylight.

Week 3: Camera Movements and Final Touches

Plan and animate camera paths to showcase different angles of the house and courtyard. Refine details like textures, lighting, and camera movements to make the scene more immersive. Render the final sequence and review the results to ensure everything looks polished.
